About the app

Translation App is built to do one thing well: turn text from one language into another, quickly and clearly. Type a word, a sentence, or a longer passage, choose where it's going, and the translation appears in moments — no sign-up, no clutter, just the words you need.

It's designed for the moments where language gets in the way: comparing notes with classmates, ordering food somewhere the menu isn't in your language, or working with a colleague on the other side of the world. With 21 languages across four script families, and more on the way, understanding is always just one translation away.
